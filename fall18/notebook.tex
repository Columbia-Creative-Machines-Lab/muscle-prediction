    \subsection{Fix stationarity (period).}\label{fix-stationarity-period.}

Mean, variance, and autocorrelation of each cycle are not quite
constant. We can't usefully determine correlation between variables
until stationarity is maintained. We can eliminate stationarity by
differencing data: \[
Y_i = Z_i - Z_{i-1} \quad \forall i\in\big(0, \dots,T\big)
\] or by fitting a trend and removing residuals: \[
Y = a + bln\,X
\] We'd also like to enforce constant period (and add our detrended
period back in later) for robust regression: \[
P_t = P_{t+1} \quad \forall t \in\big(0, (T-1)\big)
\] First, adjust for seasonality.
